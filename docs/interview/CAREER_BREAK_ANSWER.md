# Career Break Interview Answer

Use this answer honestly and calmly.

## Short Answer

"I had a career break, and now I am fully focused on returning as a
Python/Django developer. I used this preparation time to rebuild my basics,
practice Python, SQL, Django, Django REST Framework, and build a real tiffin
order management project. I am ready for a junior backend role where I can
contribute and continue improving."

## Stronger Project-Based Answer

"During my break, I decided to seriously prepare for backend development. I
created a structured learning plan and practiced Python fundamentals, SQL,
Django, REST APIs, testing, and deployment basics. My main project is a Tiffin
Order Management System where I implemented models, authentication, order
management, REST APIs, and project documentation. This helped me rebuild my
confidence and become ready for Django developer interviews."

## What Not To Say

- Do not apologize too much.
- Do not say "I forgot everything."
- Do not say "I will learn after joining."
- Do not sound unsure about your return.

## Best Tone

Be honest, calm, and practical.

You are not hiding the gap. You are showing preparation after the gap.
