## Final Readiness Rule

This course can prepare you strongly for junior Python/Django interviews if you
do three things:

1. Complete the daily practice files.
2. Build the final tiffin project.
3. Practice explaining answers out loud.

Reading alone is not enough. Building and explaining is what makes you
interview-ready.
